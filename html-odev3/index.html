Chocolate Mosaic Cake

Turkish Mosaic cake is a very practical and delicious dessert to make.

5 ingredients are needed for this cake, which is very popular with its recipe made with chocolate pudding: Biscuit, butter, milk, sugar and cocoa. Easy Mosaic Cake recipe and all the tips you need in this recipe! Bon Appetit.

Difficulty: Medium

Servings: 4-6 people

Preparation: 20 mins

Cooking: 20 mins

Total: 40 mins

Ingredients:

  • 0.6 pound of Petit-Beurre cookie
  • 2 egg
  • 1/2 tablespoon of flour
  • 1 cup of water
  • 4 tablespoons of hazelnut
  • 6 tablespoons of granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of cacao
  • 1 pack of vanillin
  • 1/2 tablespoon of butter

Directions

  1. In a saucepan, melt the butter.
  2. Put eggs, cocoa, sugar, flour in the mixing bowl and combine all ingredients. Slowly add 1 cup of water. Cook over low heat.
  3. After mixing all the ingredients well, turn off the stove. When it becomes warm, add the petit beurre biscuits and beaten hazelnuts to the bowl and mix gently with a spatula.
  4. Spread it longitudinally over the baking tray and wrap roll or pyramid shape. Keep in the freezer section of the refrigerator. Serve by slicing.
